LearnTurkish AI - Account Deletion Request

We're sorry to see you go. You can request to delete your LearnTurkish AI account and all associated personal data through this page or directly within the app.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your profile information (name, email, profile photo)
  • All learning progress (XP, streak, completed lessons, achievements)
  • Chat history with AI coaches
  • TÖMER preparation progress and exam attempts
  • Push notification preferences and device tokens
  • Custom flashcards and saved vocabulary
  • All user-generated content

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: From Within the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the LearnTurkish AI app
  2. Tap the Profile tab
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon)
  4. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision in the warning dialog
  6. Your account will be permanently deleted

Option 2: Email Request

Send your account deletion request to:

Please include in your email:

  • Your registered email address

  • Confirmation that you understand this action is permanent
✓ Processing Time: We will confirm your request within 48 hours and complete deletion within 30 days.
⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose all your learning progress, achievements, premium features, and any saved content.

Active Subscriptions

If you have an active Premium or TÖMER Pro subscription, please cancel it before requesting deletion:

  • iOS users: Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → LearnTurkish AI
  • Android users: Google Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel subscriptions managed by Apple or Google.

Data Retention

After your deletion request is processed:

  • Active personal data will be deleted within 30 days
  • Backup copies will be removed within 90 days
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained for service improvement
  • Financial transaction records may be retained as required by law (up to 7 years)
