Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

LearnTurkish AI ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by MK Edtech OÜ, registered in Estonia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Email address, name, and profile photo when you sign up via email, Google, or Apple.
  • Usage data: App activity, learning progress, XP points, streaks, and quiz results.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version for technical support purposes.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Paddle (our Merchant of Record). We do not store credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the LearnTurkish AI learning experience.
  • To track your progress and generate certificates.
  • To send push notifications about daily challenges and streaks (you can opt out anytime).
  • To improve our AI coaching and quiz content.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions via Paddle.

3. AI and Data Processing

LearnTurkish AI uses Anthropic's Claude API to power AI conversations. Your chat messages are sent to Anthropic's servers for processing. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle data.

Voice features are powered by ElevenLabs. Audio is processed on ElevenLabs servers. See ElevenLabs' Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure hosted on AWS. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with the following service providers:

  • Anthropic — AI conversation processing
  • ElevenLabs — Text-to-speech audio
  • Supabase — Database and authentication
  • Paddle — Payment processing
  • Expo / EAS — App delivery and push notifications

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

As we are registered in Estonia (EU), we comply with GDPR. You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@learnturkishai.com.

7. Children's Privacy

LearnTurkish AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for session management and analytics. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
