Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

LearnTurkish AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: email address, name, and language preferences. We also collect usage data such as XP, streak, quiz results, and chat history to personalize your learning experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the app, personalize your learning experience, send optional push notifications, and process payments via Paddle.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL). We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services: Anthropic (AI responses), ElevenLabs (text-to-speech), Supabase (database), Paddle (payments), and Expo (app infrastructure).

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Profile screen in the app.

Children's Privacy

LearnTurkish AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: support@learnturkishai.com
